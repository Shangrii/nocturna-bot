<objective>
Consolidate `config.py` so its 19 safe tunables are read at the point of use through the store,
with zero call-site edits across the cogs, while every secret and structural value stays frozen.
Wire the one-time startup seed in `bot.py::main()`.

Purpose: CONF-01 (read-at-use) + CONF-02 (secrets/structural unaffected) + STORE-05 startup wiring.
This is the behavior-preserving switch: after it, a stored edit takes effect on the next read, but
until an owner edits anything the bot behaves byte-identically (the seed writes the same .env values).
Output: an extended `config.py` (a PEP 562 module `__getattr__` shim + `_SAFE_TUNABLE_KEYS`) and a
one-line seed call in `bot.py`. Turns the four cog read-at-use tests green and keeps the full suite green.
</objective>
